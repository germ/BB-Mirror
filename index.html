Artist Statement

Handcrafted Artisanal Emoji

A project to explore dialogue using non-linear statements and image. Wow, that sounds like bullcrap. The Feedback Loop is a playground, where you poke the box and the box pokes back.

In English: I'm lonely and want to talk. Join me?

Hackers are not painters

I am a Painter, Poet, Muralist, Programmer, Collagist, API-Glueman. These are my tools I will use to converse with you. What will be your voice? Simple english, perhaps a beautiful section of code, a core dump? Shall we have a friendly argument or liken monads and orchestra?

Art API

At BlackBox we believe that software runs the metamodern world, so why not integrate ourselves into it? An API for crafting works from fragments floating through the datacenter, your terminal, IoT connected cat or otherwise. Graphs are so 2009, let us interpret those sales reports and place a wonderful object on your office walls.

With a Go Library and JSON API, you can glue BlackBox into any project. What isn't Art?
